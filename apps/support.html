Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All data — examination results, confession records, prayer sessions, patron saint dates — is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing leaves your device. See the full privacy policy.

How do I transfer my data to a new device?

Data is stored locally on your device. To transfer it, ensure iCloud Backup is enabled on your old device, then restore from that backup on your new device. The app data will be included in the backup.

I deleted the app and lost my data. Can it be recovered?

If you had iCloud Backup enabled before deleting the app, restoring from that backup may recover your data. Otherwise, locally stored data cannot be recovered after the app is deleted.

The app is crashing or behaving unexpectedly.

Please email apps@sienafoundation.org with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened. Screenshots are helpful if you can capture them.

Is the app free?

Yes. Yoke is free with no in-app purchases and no subscription.

Will there be an Android version?

Not at present. The app is iOS only.
