Scripture

Scripture texts are taken from the Douay-Rheims Bible, an English translation of the Latin Vulgate completed in the sixteenth and seventeenth centuries and revised by Bishop Richard Challoner in the eighteenth century. The Douay-Rheims text is in the public domain.

Summa Theologiae

References and citations from the Summa Theologiae of St. Thomas Aquinas are drawn from the public domain Latin text and standard English translations. The philosophical and theological framework of the examination content is derived from Aquinas's moral theology, which is in the public domain.

Liturgical Texts

Prayers and liturgical texts from the traditional Roman Rite (1962 Missal) are in the public domain. English translations of liturgical prayers included in the app are original translations by the Siena Foundation or are in the public domain.
